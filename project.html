I'VE ALWAYS BEEN A BIG BASKETBALL GUY, FOLLOW ALONG TO SEE MY JOURNEY!!


This graph as you see below was my inspiration to play basketball.
I always wanted to be like all of these players growing up, especially LeBron James (he is my sunshine).

Elementary School

I picked up basketball in 3rd grade.
I developed a love for the game and always wanted to improve!




Middle School

In 7th grade, I tried out and made the school basketball team!
I was super pumped. My NBA dreams were in sight!

At least I thought. I ended up quitting basketball after middle school. Let me explain why:

  1. I had a bad ankle injury
  2. I hated the coaches:
    • They only got mad when you messed up instead of telling you what to fix
    • Didn't show a love for the game
    • Had low basketball IQ


Since I quit basketball, I didn't do any sports in high school but was still a big NCAA and NBA fan.


College

In college, I've continued to be a huge fan. I sat front row for every single home basketball game (cowboy hat).
I even traveled to Kansas City to watch BYU in the Big 12 tournament!

And despite quitting many years ago, I still love to shoot around and play pickup games with my friends.
